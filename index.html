Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about FolioAI.

Does FolioAI send my documents to the cloud?
No. Every feature in FolioAI runs entirely on your Mac using Apple's on-device AI frameworks. Your files never leave your device. There are no servers, no cloud processing, and no third-party AI services involved — not ChatGPT, not OpenAI, not anyone else.
How do I extract action items from a meeting document?
Create a collection, add your meeting notes or transcript, then open the Actions tab. FolioAI automatically identifies and lists every commitment, task, and deadline — sorted by priority. You can also see which document each action came from.
Can FolioAI work completely offline?
Yes. After the initial AI model download (which happens once on first launch), FolioAI works entirely offline. Analysis, chat, briefings, and risk detection all run on your Mac's Neural Engine with no internet connection.
What is a Collection in FolioAI?
A Collection is a smart workspace for a project, client, or topic. You add documents to it — meeting notes, PDFs, contracts, reports — and FolioAI analyses them together. This lets it find patterns, contradictions, and insights across your entire body of documents, not just one file at a time.
How is FolioAI different from ChatGPT or Claude?
ChatGPT and Claude send your documents to remote servers operated by OpenAI and Anthropic. FolioAI runs entirely on your Mac — your contracts, meeting notes, and client files never leave your device. It's the private choice for lawyers, consultants, executives, and anyone handling confidential information.
What Mac does FolioAI require?
FolioAI requires a Mac with Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, M4 or later) running macOS 14 Sonoma or later. Apple Silicon's Neural Engine is what makes fast, fully offline AI processing possible.
Can I try FolioAI before paying?
Yes. FolioAI comes with a 7-day free trial that gives full access to every Pro feature. No credit card is charged until the trial ends. You can cancel at any time through System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
How do I generate a briefing from my documents?
Open a Collection, go to the Briefings tab, and click Generate. FolioAI reads all documents in the collection and produces a structured briefing with key points, decisions, and open questions — in seconds, entirely on your Mac.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, tap FolioAI, and select Cancel Subscription. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period. Apple handles all billing — FolioAI never stores your payment information.
